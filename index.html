What is This Website?

AAEC ResConn is organized by graduate students, for graduate students. Its goal is to bring together graduate students from UGA. The Meetup will consist of 20 minute talks given mostly by graduate students, and two plenary talks from invited speakers. It’s got a page for registration, where you can embed a Google Form or some other way for participants to register. It’s got a program page to list the schedule for the conference. It’s got a page to put up the flyers and other promotional materials for folks to download.
